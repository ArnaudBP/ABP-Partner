# Prompts Midjourney pour ABP Partner

Voici une liste de prompts optimisés pour générer des images ultra-réalistes et stylées pour votre nouveau site. Copiez-collez ces textes dans Midjourney.

## 1. Image Hero (Bandeau principal)
**Description :** Une cuisine ultra-moderne, luxueuse, tons sombres et dorés.
**Prompt :**
> cinematic wide shot of an ultra-luxury modern kitchen, matte black cabinets with gold brass handles, large white marble island with waterfall edge, warm ambient led lighting under cabinets, floor to ceiling windows with view of a city at twilight, hyper-realistic, 8k, architectural photography, interior design magazine style, dramatic lighting --ar 16:9 --v 6.0

## 2. Section "À Domicile" (Concept)
**Description :** Un designer travaillant sur une tablette dans un salon moderne.
**Prompt :**
> professional interior designer man in smart casual suit sketching on an iPad Pro, standing in a bright modern living room, blurred background showing a happy couple looking at samples, high depth of field, professional photography, soft natural lighting, 8k --ar 4:3 --v 6.0

## 3. Section "Votre Projet" (3D / Technologie)
**Description :** Gros plan sur un écran montrant un rendu 3D photoréaliste.
**Prompt :**
> close-up shot of a high-tech computer monitor displaying a photorealistic 3D wireframe render of a kitchen cabinet, digital blueprint overlay, depth of field, blue and orange lighting accents, tech atmosphere, 8k, detailed texture --ar 16:9 --v 6.0

## 4. Portfolio - Cuisine Casher / Grand Luxe
**Description :** Grande cuisine blanche, double équipements, très épurée.
**Prompt :**
> spacious luxury kosher kitchen design, pristine white high-gloss cabinets, double stainless steel sinks, double ovens, minimalist style, very clean lines, bright daylight, marble floor, high-end appliances, architectural digest style, 8k --ar 3:2 --v 6.0

## 5. Portfolio - Optimisation Espace (Petit appartement)
**Description :** Cuisine compacte mais chic, optimisation intelligente.
**Prompt :**
> clever compact modern kitchen design in a small Parisian apartment, smart storage solutions, light oak wood cabinets mixed with sage green, cozy atmosphere, soft morning light, interior design photography, highly detailed, 8k --ar 3:2 --v 6.0

## 6. Portfolio - Ambiance Sombre & Chic (Défi Lumière)
**Description :** Cuisine sombre, éclairage artificiel dramatique.
**Prompt :**
> moody modern kitchen design, dark grey matte finish, dramatic artificial LED lighting strips integrated into shelves, black backsplash, sleek and masculine design, cinematic lighting, shadows and contrast, 8k, photorealistic --ar 3:2 --v 6.0

## 7. Section Conseil (Business)
**Description :** Ambiance bureau moderne, consulting.
**Prompt :**
> modern corporate meeting room, glass walls, blurred silhouette of professionals shaking hands, abstract business concept, blue and white tones, clean, professional atmosphere, high quality stock photography style --ar 16:9 --v 6.0

---
**Conseils pour Midjourney :**
- Utilisez `--ar 16:9` pour les images larges (bannières).
- Utilisez `--ar 3:2` ou `--ar 4:3` pour les images de contenu.
- Ajoutez `--style raw` si vous voulez un rendu moins "artistique" et plus "photo".
